    Return Policy

    "At Mizuno, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free return policy. You may return any unused or defective item within 2-3 days of receipt for a full refund or exchange. To initiate a return, simply contact our customer service team with your order number and the reason for return. Once your return request is approved, we will provide you with a prepaid shipping label for easy return shipping. Please ensure that the item is returned in its original packaging and condition to qualify for a refund or exchange. Once we receive your returned item, we will process your refund or exchange promptly. Please note that shipping fees are non-refundable, and return shipping costs may apply unless the return is due to a defect or error on our part. If you have any questions or concerns about our return policy, please don't hesitate to contact us. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we're here to help make your shopping experience as enjoyable as possible.
